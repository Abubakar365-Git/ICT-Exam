About Harvard University

Harvard University, established in 1636, is the oldest institution of higher education in the United States. Located in Cambridge, Massachusetts, it is recognized for academic excellence, cutting-edge research, and its long-standing history of producing global leaders.

Academics

Harvard offers a variety of undergraduate, graduate, and professional programs to foster intellectual development and innovation. It is home to world-renowned faculty and resources that enable students to excel academically.

Admissions

Join a diverse community of passionate scholars. Our admissions process is designed to identify those who will make the most of the Harvard experience and contribute meaningfully to the world.

Research

Harvard leads the world in research across disciplines, from medical breakthroughs to innovations in engineering, social sciences, and humanities.

Campus Life

Experience the vibrant campus life at Harvard, where traditions meet modernity. Participate in student clubs, events, and initiatives that enrich your time here.
